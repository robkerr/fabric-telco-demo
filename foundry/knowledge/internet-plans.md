# Home Internet Plans

TelcoConnect offers four residential internet tiers. Prices are monthly recurring charges (MRC)
and exclude equipment and taxes.

| Plan | Download | Upload | MRC | Best for |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Internet 100 | 100 Mbps | up to 20 Mbps | $45 | Browsing, HD streaming, 1–3 devices |
| Internet 500 | 500 Mbps | up to 100 Mbps | $65 | 4K streaming, gaming, many devices |
| Internet Gig | 1,000 Mbps | up to 200 Mbps | $85 | Heavy households, work-from-home |
| Internet 2 Gig | 2,000 Mbps | up to 500 Mbps | $110 | Power users, creators, home labs |

## Equipment

- **TelcoConnect Modem** — $5/mo. Required for cable/DSL service unless you provide your own.
- **TelcoConnect Mesh Router** — $10/mo. Whole-home Wi‑Fi 6 coverage; recommended for Gig+ plans.
- Bring your own compatible modem/router to avoid rental fees.

## Availability

Speeds depend on the technology available at your address (Fiber, Cable, DSL, or Fixed Wireless).
Fiber is required for the 2 Gig plan. Check availability by ZIP code before ordering.

## Activation

New installations include a one-time **$35 activation fee** and a partial-month proration on the
first bill. This is why a first bill is often higher than the recurring monthly charge.
