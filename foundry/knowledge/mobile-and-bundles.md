# Mobile Plans & Bundles

TelcoConnect mobile is available standalone or bundled with home internet.

| Plan | Data | MRC | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Mobile Unlimited | Unlimited | $55 | Premium data, hotspot included |
| Mobile 40GB | 40 GB | $40 | High-speed data cap, then throttled |
| Mobile 10GB | 10 GB | $25 | Light users |

## Bundle savings

- **Add Mobile, Save $10/mo** (`PROMO_XSELL_1`): existing internet customers who add a mobile
  line get $10/mo off the mobile MRC.
- **Bundle TV + Internet, Save 15%** (`PROMO_XSELL_2`): 15% off when Streaming TV is added to
  an internet plan.

## Devices

- **Aurora 5G Phone** — $22/mo over 24 months.
- **Aurora Lite Phone** — $12/mo over 24 months.
- Bring your own device (BYOD) — no device charge.

## Eligibility

Bundle discounts require an active internet account in good standing. Discounts apply on the
next full billing cycle after the mobile line activates.
