# Service, Outages & Retention Offers

## Service health

TelcoConnect monitors per-account service quality: uptime %, latency (ms), and packet loss (%).
Healthy broadband typically shows uptime ≥ 99.5%, latency < 45 ms, and packet loss < 1%.

## Outages

Outages are tracked by area (ZIP/region) with a severity (Minor, Major, Critical) and a root
cause (fiber cut, power loss, equipment failure, weather, congestion). During an active outage in
your area, service metrics degrade (higher latency, lower uptime). Weather-related outages can be
corroborated with local weather conditions.

## Work orders & appointments

Repairs may be resolved remotely or require a technician visit (truck roll). Appointment windows
are typically 8:00 AM–12:00 PM. Customers are notified of technician name and window.

## Reconnection

A suspended account (usually for non-payment) is reconnected after the past-due balance is paid.
Reconnection is typically completed within a short window after payment posts.
